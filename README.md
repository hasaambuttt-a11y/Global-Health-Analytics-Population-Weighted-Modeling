# Global-Health-Analytics-Population-Weighted-Modeling


## Goal: 
Analyze longitudinal trends in BMI, Diabetes, and Hypertension to identify regional health risks and strategic investment priorities.  

## Technical Stack: 
Power BI, DAX, Python, Star Schema Modeling.  

## Key Methodology: 
Designed a Star Schema to integrate population data with regional health metrics across 200 countries.  
Developed a Population-Weighted Impact Analysis to move the narrative from simple prevalence percentages to absolute human volume.  

## Strategic Findings:
Identified the "European Anomaly," where advanced medical interventions successfully decoupled rising BMI from high blood pressure.  
Determined that while Oceania faces the highest intensive clinical percentages, Asia represents a massive-scale logistical crisis due to population volume.  

## Outcome:
Provided a strategic roadmap for tiered health investments based on regional needs rather than flat percentages.
